Project Arya

Innovative Drone technology for Public Safety

In Development

Problem & Solution

Problem

Indian soldiers face significant risks in combat because they often struggle to determine the direction of enemy gunfire precisely. Civilians and VIPs are at risk due to increasing illegal guns in India.

Solution

Six omnidirectional microphones connected to an FPGA system in a hexacopter drone detect gunshots and triangulate their source using TDOA. The data is shown on a real-time map interface.
